### All Roles ###
- Pooling resources and bursting together as a party will always yield the best results.
- **Resistance Reraisers** should be applied at all times. Having one on gives you a 70% (or 80%) chance of being revived immediately on death.
- **Light Curtains** are useful during Queen's Guard, Stygimoloch Lord, and The Queen to reflect various AoEs.
- If you are looking for resources to improve at your job, please visit <a href="https://www.thebalanceffxiv.com/" target="_blank">The Balance</a> website.
