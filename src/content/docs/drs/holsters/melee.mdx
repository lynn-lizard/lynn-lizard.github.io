:::caution[How To Mimic]
<ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>**Mimic** is used to copy the actions of another player _and_ fully refresh your actions at the same time! To use it, first slot in <ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>**Mimic** and target the player you wish to copy the actions from.
**Before you use <ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>Mimic**, double check their **Reminiscence** buff! Doing this will ensure you are getting the correct actions. 

Once ready, use <ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>**Mimic!** After a nifty animation, you'll recieve **both** actions the target had and full charges of both!
:::
