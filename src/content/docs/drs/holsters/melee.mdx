Melee DPS are the powerhouses of Delubrum Reginae. Along with being some of the highest DPS roles, they are given arguably the strongest (and easiest) to use actions.
Both actions should be combined together every 2 minute window to maximize their effectiveness.

Each holster is split into two sections, one as a "pre-load"  holster and a "instance ready" holster. The "pre-load" is for when you first get into the instance. It will contain your pure essence, reraiser, and actions that you will slot in or refresh charges on.
The "instance ready" holster has _everything_ you will need for the entire instance. It should often contain extra reraisers, spare copies of lost actions you need, and light curtains. It is strongly encouraged that you fill your holster to as close as full as you can.

Some suggestions on what to include to fill space: **Light Curtains**, extra **Resistance Reraisers**, and **Resistance Potions**.

Your **Pure Essence of the Duelist** synergizes with **Banner of Honored Sacrifice** to extend the banner's duration to a whopping 30 seconds! It also reduces the damage taken from ~16k to merely 1 (one) damage per tick.

:::note[Pure Essences]
You will only need **one** pure essence each time you enter Delubrum Reginae. Essences do persist through death and are only removed if you remove the buff yourself or you leave the instance.
:::

This holster is the bread of butter of a melee DPS. Using Font of Power and Banner of Honored Sacrifice together every **2 minute** in the raid buff window will yield the best results. This is the most common bag for new people to bring as it's
economic and easiest to use.

:::caution[How To Mimic]
<ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>**Mimic** is used to copy the actions of another player _and_ fully refresh your actions at the same time! To use it, first slot in <ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>**Mimic** and target the player you wish to copy the actions from.
**Before you use <ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>Mimic**, double check their **Reminiscence** buff! Doing this will ensure you are getting the correct actions. Once ready, use <ActionIcon icon="mimic"/>**Mimic!** After a nifty animation, you'll recieve **both** actions the target had and full charges of both!

**Extra note:** The target will not be effected in anyway. This is how many Delubrum Savage reclears will often smuggle heavier action combinations through the entire instance.
:::

### Excellence Melee
This is truly the easiest and most effective way to output as much DPS as you can on **any role**, outside of one exception. While this is a rather heavy and expensive bag, it's effects cannot be understated. Mimic is used before every major Non-Trinity Seeker fight to
refresh **Font of Power** and get **Lost Excellence** back. 

During the opener, you will press **Lost Excellence** after your first GCD. You will open your holster, select **Banner of Honored Sacrifice**, and slot it [the banner] over Excellence. Once done, you can press **Font of Power** and **Banner of Honored Sacrifice**
together. From there, the fight will proceed as normal with you pressing Font/Banner during every **2 minute** raid buff window.

It is important that you overslot **Excellence** with your **Banner of Honored Sacrifice**. Holding **Excellence** through the whole instance is a massive DPS loss and also could potentially waste the assigned batteries's Excellence plates.

### Excellence Melee Battery
This holster helps carry **Lost Excellence** through the entire instance. Three players will be volunteer/assigned to carry an extra plate of Excellence to slot in at an assigned fight.
One will carry it for Queen's Guard, Trinity Avowed, and The Queen. Once you have arrived at your assigned fight: slot in **Lost Excellence** over **Banner of Honored Sacrifice** (keeping **Font of Power** still) and inform the raid with a message or marking yourself with a triangle head marker to
signify that you are ready to be mimicked.

During the opener, you will press **Lost Excellence** after your first GCD. You will open your holster, select **Banner of Honored Sacrifice**, and slot it [the banner] over Excellence. Once done, you can press **Font of Power** and **Banner of Honored Sacrifice**
together. From there, the fight will proceed as normal with you pressing Font/Banner during every **2 minute** raid buff window.
