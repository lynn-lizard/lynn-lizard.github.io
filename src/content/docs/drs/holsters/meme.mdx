These are all high-level holsters that require a strong understanding of the raid to fully take advantage of. Their DPS outputs are incredibly high but they make you suspect to being one shot by mechanics and even raidwides if you are not attentive.
Please use these holsters with caution.

However, they are some of the most fun you can have in Delubrum (Savage). So please, if you are given the opprotunity to try one out, definitely take advantage of it!

:::danger[Non Pure Essences Ahead]
Majority of these holsters contain non-pure essence set ups. They require both Bubble and Shell 2 to function properly. All hosts do not allow these essences unless they give you
strict permission to run them. **Please ask the host before ever using any of these holsters.** 

If you are found to be using a non-pure essence with permission, you will likely be left on the ground and kicked from the run.
:::

### Irregular w/ Honored Sacrifice Overslots
This is the glass cannon of Delubrum (Savage). Irregular will help you be output as much DPS that is on par with an Excellence Melee DPS. However, you **must** mitigate everything instance of damage you take. This also includes: raidwides and any damage from mechanics.
It is commonly suggested to use your short cooldown along with another cooldown like rampart, your 30%, or your other personal mitigations.

During your opener, while building **Blood Rage** stacks, you would swap into **Honored Sacrifice** to help increase your DPS output. When under **Blood Rush**, make sure to be spaamming both **Font of Power** and **Honored Sacrifice** on cooldown to get as many extra uses as possible. Drifting them back into the **2 minute**
buff window for any future bursts.

The 2 **Impetus** plates are used for TA and Queen to accerlate your enterance into the **Blood Rush** window. You would slot it after you activate **Blood Rage**, use **Impetus**, slot in **Honored Sacrifice**, and then dash twice to enter **Blood Rush** during the 2 minute buff window.

:::danger[Fortitudes, Bubble and Shell 2]
You are REQUIRED to have 8 stacks of Fortitude, Bubble, and Shell 2. Not having these will often result in instantaneous death. 
:::

You can also swap out the contents of this holster for something akin to Impetus chaining while still using Irregular. If you're looking for more on how to Impetus chain, please see the [Impetus Chaining Guide](/drs/bloodrage/#impetus-chaining)

### Profane Healer
Ditch the utility and become the angry healer you've always wanted to be. You will become essentially a DPS now and should act like one. Your one job is to deal as much damage as possible. Doing "Chainspell openers"
to help maximize as damage as you can is key to performing well. You should have a **Full Cure Healer** helping support your MP costs as well as a **Cure 2** phys. ranged too.

During your opener you will press **Chainspell** then slot **Seraph Strike** over it. From there you will press both **Font of Magic** and **Seraph Strike** together during every **2 minute** raid buff window.
Remember to use **Thin Air** if you're on WHM!

:::danger[Bubble and Shell 2]
You are REQUIRED to have Bubble and Shell 2. Not having these will often result in instantaneous death. 
:::

### Profane Chainspell Battery

to help maximize as damage as you can is key to performing well. You should have a **Full Cure Healer** helping support your MP costs as well as a **Cure 2** phys. ranged too.

Used to help carry **Chainspell** through the whole instance. At your assigned fight, you will slot in Chainspell over the **Non-Font of Magic** action and mark yourself with a **+** head marker signifying you are ready to be mimicked.

During your opener you will press **Chainspell** then slot **Seraph Strike** over it. From there you will press both **Font of Magic** and **Seraph Strike** together during every **2 minute** raid buff window.
Remember to use **Thin Air** if you're on WHM!
