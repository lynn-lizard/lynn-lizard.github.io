### Chemist Cheesing
Sometimes you wipe, it happens. What's important is setting up a Chemist Cheese so you can give your group another attempt at whatever boss you're currently on. To perform this 2 chemists must do the following:
1. Any Chemist purposefully dies, either by mechanics or jumping into a death wall.
2. Another Chemist gives that dead Chemist a raise. The dead chemist will hold the raise until the boss resets completely.
   - Try to cast raise near the enterance of the arena! The raise position is deteremined where the raise-giver was standing. So having the raise-caster be out of a boss's pull range will make raising safer later.
3. After the raise is confirmed on the dead chemist, everyone will quickly wipe. Either by jumping in the wall or otherwise dying.
4. After the boss completely resets, the dead chemist will revive.
5. The now alive chemist will raise everyone outside of the boss's pull range.
6. Wait for weaknesses to fall off and pull again!

This can also be useful for stopping some folks for re-leveling. Sometimes there's just not enough people for another attempt so raising as many people as possible then just `/return`-ing out can
save on re-leveling time.
