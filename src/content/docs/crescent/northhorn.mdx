# LiveLetter Teasers and Information
:::danger[Unknown Information]
Any information not listed here is either speculative, rumored, or false.
:::

Link to relevant sections of LiveLetter 91: https://www.youtube.com/live/JxR-gTLJmzM?si=k-HdMBE6Tch7dWY8
- Timestamps: 1:23:00 to 1:35:30

Coming sometime in the 7.5 patch cycle, Occult Crescent: North Horn will feature an entirely new zone with new a locale, FATEs, engagements, Phantom Weapons, Occult Gear, 
and the second half of the Forked Tower dungeon series.

## The TL;DR
- Knowledge Level cap increased to 40!
- New Mobs/Monsters, FATEs, Critical Engagements
- New Phantom Jobs, so far the only ones to be mentioned/shown so far are...
   - Black Mage
   - White Mage
   - Summoner
   - Red Mage
   - Dragoon
   - Ninja
   - Blue Mage
   - Necromancer
- New sets of Occult Gear
  - Notably: If you have Arcanaut's+2 gear, you will have the ability to "skip" some steps when enhancing your gear in North Horn.
- New 48 person dungeon/raid - The Forked Tower: Magic.
- Two difficulties of Forked Tower: Magic
  - A more casual "Normal" mode geared towards story enjoyers.
    - Queued through joining a North Horn instance and standing on the Aetherial Node when active.
    - If not selected for an entry, you will be given priority for the next (think Notoriety from Bozja Duels?)
  - A harder, "Extreme" version for those seeking a challenge.
    - Queue into an "extreme-mode-only" instance with Forked Tower immediately accessible

## Gearing in North Horn
More grinds for more gear! You will get the ability to further enhance your gear to become even stronger than you currently are. There are items likely similar to the fixatives and 
aetherspun silver/gold needing to be gathered upgrade your gear.

If you currently have any upgraded gear from the South Horn, you will be able to skip some of the steps required to enhance/obtain gear from North Horn. Hope you have your Arcanaut's+2 ready!

## Forked Tower: Magic
Rejoice! There are now two difficulties. The "Normal" version for those who wish to experience the story in a more casual manner and the more challenging "Extreme" version for those who want something more.

A maximum of 48 players can join either version of the dungeon.

Ciphers are dead! No more ciphers!!

### Normal Mode
Queued similar to Forked Tower: Blood, Castrum Lacus Litore (Bozja), Dalriada (Zadnor), and/or other Critical Engagements. 
Standing atop the Aetherial Node will mark you as "interested" to join the duty so when the recruitment time ends.

If more than 48 are present, players will be selected at random. There seems to be no lower player limit, so you could potentially queue in solo!

If not selected to join, you will be given priority for your next attempt (similar to Notoriety from Bozja Duels?)

### Extreme Mode
Can be entered by queueing into an "extreme-mode-only" instance, which are different from the normal instances. These unique instances may be entered by a minimum of **12 players** in 3 or more parties.

Once inside this "extreme only" insatnce, Forked Tower: Magic (Extreme) will be immediately accessible for entry.
