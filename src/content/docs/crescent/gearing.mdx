## Gearing in Occult Crescent
Each Field Operation features a new armor set that has its own unique set of effects specific to that Field Op’s zone. 
In Occult Crescent, we have ilvl 745 **Arcanaut’s Gear** and **Occult Accessories** which feature main stat bonuses and a stat called Special Attribute.

**Special Attribute increases Phantom Action damage by ~1% per point.**

**Each piece of occult gear also receives +40/80/120 main stat.** Main stat increases your overall damage by a much more significant 
margin than other stats in the game. Always prioritize wearing Arcanaut’s or Occult Accessories over trying to reach a certain stat tier or specific GCD.

:::note[Deep Occult Rings]
The English translation on the Deep Rings is misleading, as it requires 5 unique accessories to be worn to receive its effects.

This is explained in more detail in the [Best in Slot section](gearing#armor-and-accessories)
:::

### Materia Melding and You
#### Don’t! Materia affixed to gear higher than ilvl 700 is disabled in Occult Crescent.
In FFXIV, if a piece of gear’s item level is above the duty’s item level sync,all materia on the gear will be disabled. This is consistent with all other duties in the game.

This means, while occult gear can be melded, it will not recieve any benefits from the melded materia while in Occult Crescent. 
The only gear that can be melded and still have those melds function is the Neo Kingdom gearset.

## Where to Obtain Occult Gear
### Arcanaut's Armor
You can purchase all 7 sets of Arcanaut’s Gear from the Expedition Antiquarian at the Base Camp for 4,000 
enlightened silver pieces per armor piece. There is no specific purchase order as each piece costs the same 
amount of silver and provides an equal amount of bonus stats.

### Occult Accessories
These are very rarely obtained from completing Critical Encounters, opening bronze/silver 
treasure coffers, and opening gold chests from either a Persistent Pot or Happy Bunnies (Fortune Carrots).

#### Accessory Sources and Drop Rates
- **Earrings, Necklaces, and Bracelets** can only be obtained from Critical Encounters and treasure coffers 
around the South Horn. Specific CEs drop specific (non-ring) gear slots, either ‘of Blood’ or ‘of Magic’ at random. A map showcasing which CE drops which accessory is shown below:
  - CEs have a ~0.1% drop rate (our best estimated value)
  - Coffers have a ~0.06% drop rate.

## Upgrading Occult Gear
For now, you can purchase upgrade materials from Expedition Antiquarian for silver, gold, and sanguinite (from Forked Tower: Blood).

Each upgrade increases your main stat by +40 and grants one point of special attribute. Below is a list of required 
materials you must present, along with the armor piece itself, to the Expedition Armorer in Base Camp in order to 
upgrade the armor piece. Currently, Occult Accessories cannot be upgraded.

## Occult Crescent Best-in-Slot
The Best in Slot for **all** jobs/roles is wearing a full set of Arcanaut’s +2 gear and all 5 unique Occult 
Accessories (Earring, Necklace, Bracelet, Ring, and Deep Ring).

The main stat and Special Attribute bonuses will provide the highest damage gain for minimal effort. 
The bonus main stat will far, far outweigh any specific substat tiering or GCD speeds possible as every 40 
main stat will increase your general rotation’s damage by ~1%. Main stat bonuses will not affect Phantom Action 
damage, however, the Special Attribute bonus will increase Phantom Action damage by ~1% per point.

### Armor and Accessories
When starting out, you should wear as many pieces of Arcanaut’s Gear and Occult Accessories as you can reasonably obtain. By the end, you should work 
towards obtaining Arcanaut’s +2 Gear and all 5 unique Occult Accessories.

If you do not have a piece of Occult gear for a specific gear slot, fill it with the highest item level piece with desirable substats.

:::tip[Wearing Two Non-Deep Rings]
The English translation on the Deep Rings is misleading. It requires 5 unique accessories to be worn to receive its effects.

This means if you do not have all 5 unique accessories, you could temporarily wear two non-deep rings. Wearing two non-deep rings does grant their 
bonus effects for a total of +80 main stat and +2 Special Attribute.

However, both rings will be spiritbond to you; this means when you do get all 5 accessories, the extra non-deep ring will essentially be useless and untradeable.
:::

### Weapons
Phantom Weapons are generally the safest pick! They provide capped desirable substats at ilvl 700. 
There are some cases when another weapon may be better, but these differences are so minimal that it’s 
not worth worrying over. A good rule of thumb is to use the highest ilvl weapon with desirable stats.

### Foods and Tinctures 
Both work in Occult Crescent! The best options are the current raid tier’s consumables 
for your job/role (e.g. 7.4 is Caramel Popcorn and Grade 4 Gemdraught).

Note: Tinctures/Gemdraughts do not increase Phantom Action damage.


### What about Neo Kingdom?
Since occult gear now occupies every slot except for weapons and due to how powerful the gear's bonuses are, 
Neo Kingdom gear has been made irrelevant. If you're still working on getting your occult gear, 
neo kingdom can help you gain marginally small fractions \(\<1\%\) of damage gain, 
but its easier to just bring in the highest ilvl pieces with your role's preferred stats.

Though with how quickly you would be replacing this gear, it's strongly advised to not invest in Neo Kingdom.
